Blood Group Identification System

Revolutionizing blood type detection through fingerprint analysis

Project Overview

Our innovative system leverages deep learning and computer vision techniques to establish a correlation between fingerprint patterns and blood groups. This non-invasive approach offers a quick and efficient alternative to traditional blood typing methods.
